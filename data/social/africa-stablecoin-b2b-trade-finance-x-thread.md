# X Thread - africa-stablecoin-b2b-trade-finance

Tweet 1 (Hook):
127 African banks lost correspondent banking relationships in 2024-2025. The banks that cut them are not coming back. Africa's $74B trade finance gap is not a credit problem. It is an infrastructure exit. 🧵

Tweet 2 (Context):
Most coverage of African stablecoin adoption treats it as a remittance story. Diaspora sending money home. That market is real. But it is not where the structural shift is happening.

Tweet 3 (Insight):
B2B stablecoin payment volume grew from under $100M/month in early 2023 to over $6B/month by mid-2025. That is 60x in 30 months. Cost: 0.1-0.5% on stablecoin rails vs 2-7% through correspondent banking.

Tweet 4 (Opportunity):
In July 2026, Scrypt expanded stablecoin settlement rails across Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, and Uganda. Ripple joined Flutterwave to build stablecoin payments infrastructure across Africa. The settlement layer for intra-African trade is being built now.

Tweet 5 (CTA):
