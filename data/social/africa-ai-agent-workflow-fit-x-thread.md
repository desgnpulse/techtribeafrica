Tweet 2 (Context):
88% of African organisations are now embedding AI agents. Few ask the fit question first. The result: expensive pilots that stay pilots. The difference between a live system and a stalled pilot is not the model. It is the workflow.

Tweet 3 (Insight):
Three properties define agent-ready workflows: high-frequency volume on a channel you own, repetitive resolution patterns, and economics where human handling costs more than the transaction justifies. Pumpkn automated 70% of its workflow on WhatsApp. All three were present.

The constraint is data, not model choice. Nigeria's Federal Government saved 1.1 trillion naira in 2025 through procurement reform before the AI layer arrived. The data layer has to exist first. Agents that try to fix broken processes stay broken.
